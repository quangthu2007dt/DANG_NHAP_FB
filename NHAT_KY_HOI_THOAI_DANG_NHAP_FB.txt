NHAT KY HOI THOAI DANG NHAP FACEBOOK
===================================

1. MUC DICH FILE NAY
--------------------
- File nay duoc tao de mang sang may khac va van giu duoc luong thao luan.
- Day khong phai ban sao nguyen van toan bo chat.
- Day la ban ghi nho lai cac quy tac, quyet dinh, cach lam viec va tinh trang du an.


2. CACH LAM VIEC DA THONG NHAT
------------------------------
- Voi viec phat trien app:
  truoc khi sua code hoac tao file lien quan den logic, phai thong bao ro se lam gi.

- Voi viec backup va day GitHub:
  khong can hoi lai.
  Day la viec nen tu dong lam de giu an toan du an.

- Cach day hoc:
  huong dan tung buoc 1.
  Khong tu y lam thay truoc.
  Chi khi doan nao qua kho hoac nguoi dung yeu cau thi moi truc tiep sua code.


3. TRANG THAI DU AN O THOI DIEM DANG GHI NHAT KY
------------------------------------------------
- Du an dang thao luan va chot nghiep vu.
- Chua bat dau phat trien logic chinh theo cach moi.
- Da tung co mot buoc them scaffold ds.txt vao Form1.cs.
- Buoc do da duoc hoan tac de quay ve cach hoc "cam tay chi viec".

- Repo GitHub dang dung:
  https://github.com/quangthu2007dt/DANG_NHAP_FB


4. CAC FILE TXT QUAN TRONG DA TAO
---------------------------------
- HUONG_DAN_APP_DANG_NHAP_FB.txt
  la file huong dan chinh ve y tuong va thu tu code.

- NHAT_KY_HOI_THOAI_DANG_NHAP_FB.txt
  la file ghi nho luong trao doi va cach lam viec.


5. MUC TIEU CUA APP
-------------------
- App nay dung de dang nhap Facebook thu cong.
- Khong phai tool tu dong dang nhap hang loat.
- App chi ho tro quy trinh:
  lay tai khoan, gan profile, mo Facebook, dien san UID va Password 1 lan.
- Nguoi dung van la nguoi thao tac chinh.


6. CAC QUY TAC NGHIEP VU DA CHOT
--------------------------------

6.1. ds.txt
- File ds.txt nam canh file exe.
- Moi dong chi co dinh dang:
  uid|password

- Neu chua co ds.txt:
  app tu tao moi.

- Neu ds.txt rong:
  app thong bao va mo file de nguoi dung tu nhap.

- App khong tu dong xoa dong trong ds.txt khi bam Next.
- Chi khi nguoi dung xoa dong trong app thi moi xoa dong tuong ung trong ds.txt.


6.2. profile_mau va profile_ranh
- Co 1 profile mau duy nhat:
  ten la `profile_mau`

- Co 1 profile ranh:
  ten la `profile_ranh`

- Khi bam Next:
  neu co `profile_ranh` thi dung no.
  neu khong co thi clone moi tu `profile_mau`.

- Khi gan cho tai khoan moi:
  doi ten profile thanh dung UID.


6.3. Nut Next
- Next la nut di dong moi tu ds.txt.
- Khi bam Next:
  lay 1 uid|password moi
  gan profile
  doi ten profile thanh UID
  tao ngay 1 dong moi tren grid
  chon ngay dong moi do
  mo Facebook
  dien san uid va password 1 lan

- Next khong co nghia la dang nhap thanh cong.


6.4. Nut Chay va Mo dong
- Chay khong lay dong moi trong ds.txt.
- Chay chi mo lai dong da ton tai tren grid.
- Mo dong trong menu chuot phai co cung y nghia voi nut Chay.


6.5. Xoa dong
- Nguoi dung la nguoi tu quyet dinh xoa.
- Ly do xoa chu yeu hien tai:
  tai khoan bi bao doi mat khau.

- Neu xoa 1 dong:
  xoa dong tren grid
  xoa dong tuong ung trong ds.txt
  xoa dau vet phien dang nhap
  doi ten profile ve `profile_ranh`

- Neu xoa nhieu dong:
  da tung thao luan se xoa han profile de tranh profile tang len.
  Tuy nhien logic xoa nhieu dong chua phat trien o giai doan hien tai.
  Tam thoi xoa chi nen xem la xoa 1 dong.


6.6. Tieu chi giu hay xoa dong
- App hien tai khong can qua tap trung vao:
  dang nhap thanh cong hay that bai.

- Dieu quan trong hien tai la:
  tai khoan co bi bao doi mat khau hay khong.

- Neu khong bi bao doi mat khau:
  giu lai dong grid
  giu lai dong trong ds.txt
  giu lai profile

- Neu bi bao doi mat khau:
  xoa dong, xoa ds, reset profile

- Hien tai chua can tu tim DOM de nhan dien.
- Nguoi dung tu quan sat va tu thao tac xoa.


7. QUY TAC UI DA CHOT
---------------------
- Khi khong co dong nao duoc chon:
  Next sang
  Chay khoa
  menu theo dong khoa

- Khi chon dung 1 dong:
  Next khoa
  Chay sang
  Xoa dong sang
  menu theo dong sang

- Khi chon nhieu hon 1 dong:
  Chay khoa
  Hien tai cung khong xu ly xoa nhieu dong trong logic chinh

- Grid van cho boi den nhieu dong.
- Viec boi den nhieu dong duoc giu lai de sau nay phuc vu copy hoac cac viec khac.


8. DU LIEU TOI THIEU CUA 1 DONG MOI
-----------------------------------
- Khi bam Next, dong moi chi can co ngay:
  UID
  Password
  ProfileName = UID
  Ngay tao = hien tai

- Cac cot khac de trong:
  Ten
  Email
  Ghi chu
  Cookie
  Trang thai
  va cac cot phu khac

- Trang thai tam thoi uu tien hien o label/status duoi form.


9. NGUYEN TAC CODE DA THONG NHAT
--------------------------------
- Code se lam theo colName, khong phu thuoc thu tu cot tren grid.
- Uu tien tan dung duoc phan nao tu tao_profile thi dung lai.
- Tuy nhien phan ds.txt la logic moi, nen phai viet theo huong rieng.


10. NHUNG VIEC DA XAY RA TRONG BUOI THAO LUAN
---------------------------------------------
- Da tao mot so file txt de giu huong dan.
- Da co luc them code ds.txt vao Form1.cs.
- Sau do nguoi dung yeu cau quay ve cach day hoc tung buoc va khong lam thay.
- Code ds.txt da duoc hoan tac.
- GitHub da duoc dung de backup nhieu lan trong qua trinh.


11. TINH TRANG HIEN TAI CAN NHO
-------------------------------
- Form1.cs da duoc tra lai gan nhu ban goc de bat dau hoc lai tu dau.
- Luong nghiep vu da ro hon rat nhieu so voi luc dau.
- Chua bat dau thuc hien Buoc 2 theo cach moi.


12. BUOC TIEP THEO NEN LAM
--------------------------
- Khong viet code ngay.
- Truoc het thao luan va chot "lam cai gi dau tien".
- Theo huong da thong nhat, thu tu hop ly la:
  1. mo xe rieng luong Next
  2. sau do moi vao ds.txt
  3. roi profile_mau/profile_ranh


13. GHI CHU QUAN TRONG
----------------------
- Neu mang file nay sang may tiem, chi can doc lai file nay va file HUONG_DAN_APP_DANG_NHAP_FB.txt la co the noi lai dung luong.
- Khong can nho toan bo chat nguyen van.
