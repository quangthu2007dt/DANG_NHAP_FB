HUONG DAN APP DANG NHAP FACEBOOK
================================

1. MUC TIEU APP
---------------
- Day la app ho tro dang nhap Facebook thu cong.
- Khong phai app tu dong dang nhap hang loat.
- App chi ho tro quy trinh:
  lay tai khoan moi, gan profile, mo Facebook, dien san UID va Password 1 lan.
- Nguoi dung van la nguoi thao tac chinh trong qua trinh dang nhap.


2. CAC THANH PHAN CHINH
-----------------------
- 1 profile mau duy nhat:
  ten thu muc la `profile_mau`
  da cai san extension can dung.

- 1 profile ranh:
  ten thu muc mac dinh la `profile_ranh`
  day la profile da duoc lam sach de tai su dung.

- 1 file ds.txt:
  nam canh file exe.
  Moi dong co dinh dang:
  uid|password

- Grid trong app:
  luu cac dong tai khoan dang duoc giu lai de quan ly.

- User-Agent:
  app tu tao san danh sach de nguoi dung chon.

- Giao dien Facebook:
  co 2 lua chon de mo theo dung muc dich nguoi dung.


3. LOGIC CUA ds.txt
-------------------
- ds.txt la nguon dau vao.
- Moi dong chi co:
  uid|password

- Neu app chay ma chua co ds.txt:
  app tu tao moi ds.txt.

- Neu ds.txt rong:
  app thong bao nguoi dung nhap them.
  sau do mo luon ds.txt de nguoi dung tu nhap bang Notepad.

- App khong tu dong xoa dong trong ds.txt khi bam Next.
- Dong trong ds.txt chi bi xoa khi nguoi dung xoa dong do trong app.


4. Y NGHIA CAC NUT CHINH
------------------------

4.1. Nut Next
- Nut Next la nut di tai khoan moi.
- Khi bam Next:
  1. Lay 1 dong moi tu ds.txt.
  2. Tach thanh uid va password.
  3. Tim profile ranh ten `profile_ranh`.
  4. Neu khong co profile ranh thi tao moi tu `profile_mau`.
  5. Doi ten profile do thanh dung UID.
  6. Tao ngay 1 dong moi tren grid.
  7. Do ngay du lieu toi thieu vao dong moi.
  8. Tu dong chon dong moi do.
  9. Mo Facebook theo cau hinh da chon.
  10. Dien san uid va password 1 lan.

- Next khong phai la nut xac nhan dang nhap thanh cong.
- Next chi co nhiem vu tao 1 phien moi de nguoi dung dang nhap thu cong.


4.2. Nut Chay
- Nut Chay khong lay dong moi tu ds.txt.
- Nut Chay dung de mo lai dung dong da co tren grid.
- Tuc la mo lai profile cu cua dong dang chon.


4.3. Mo dong o menu chuot phai
- Co cung y nghia voi nut Chay.
- Tuc la chi mo lai dong da ton tai.
- Khong tao profile moi.
- Khong lay ds moi.


5. QUY TAC SANG VA KHOA NUT
---------------------------
- Khi khong co dong nao duoc chon:
  - Next sang.
  - Chay khoa.
  - Cac menu thao tac theo dong khoa.

- Khi chon dung 1 dong:
  - Next khoa.
  - Chay sang.
  - Xoa dong sang.
  - Menu thao tac theo dong sang.

- Khi chon nhieu hon 1 dong:
  - Next khoa.
  - Chay khoa.
  - Xoa dong khoa o giai doan hien tai.
  - Viec boi den nhieu dong chi de danh cho chuc nang sau nay nhu copy.

- Khi app dang xu ly dong moi bang Next:
  - Khong duoc phep bam Next tiep.


6. LOGIC CUA PROFILE
--------------------
- Luon co 1 profile mau duy nhat ten `profile_mau`.
- Profile mau khong dung truc tiep de dang nhap.
- Profile mau chi dung de tao profile moi khi can.

- Ngoai cac profile dang duoc giu lai theo tung dong, app chi can toi da 1 profile ranh ten `profile_ranh`.
- Profile ranh la profile da duoc lam sach de tai su dung.

- Khi bam Next:
  - Neu co `profile_ranh` thi dung lai profile do.
  - Neu khong co `profile_ranh` thi clone moi tu `profile_mau`.
  - Sau do doi ten profile thanh dung UID.

- Khi dong nao do duoc giu lai:
  - profile cua no cung duoc giu lai.
  - lan sau co the mo lai chinh profile do.


7. DU LIEU TOI THIEU KHI TAO DONG MOI
------------------------------------
- Grid se dung theo cac cot da thiet ke.
- Code phai thao tac theo `colName`, khong phu thuoc thu tu cot.

- Khi bam Next va tao dong moi, chi can do ngay:
  - UID
  - Password
  - ProfileName = UID
  - Ngay tao = thoi gian hien tai

- Cac cot con lai de trong:
  - Ten
  - Email
  - Ghi chu
  - Cookie
  - Trang thai
  - cac cot khac

- Trang thai hien tai uu tien hien o label/status duoi form theo kieu dien giai.
- Chua can ghi trang thai co dinh vao cot Trang thai.


8. LOGIC XOA DONG
-----------------
- Viec xoa dong hien tai do nguoi dung tu quyet dinh.
- Ly do xoa chu yeu hien tai:
  tai khoan bi bao doi mat khau.

- Neu xoa dung 1 dong:
  1. Xoa dong tren grid.
  2. Xoa dong tuong ung trong ds.txt.
  3. Xoa sach dau vet phien dang nhap Facebook cua profile do.
  4. Doi ten profile do ve `profile_ranh`.

- Neu xoa tu 2 dong tro len trong giai doan sau:
  - se xoa han cac profile tuong ung de tranh tang so luong profile.
  - nhung logic nay tam thoi chua lam ngay.

- Hien tai chua can app tu tim DOM de biet tai khoan bi doi mat khau.
- Nguoi dung la nguoi tu xac nhan va thao tac xoa.


9. KET LUAN NGHIEP VU
--------------------
- 1 dong trong grid dai dien cho 1 tai khoan dang duoc giu lai de quan ly.
- 1 thoi diem app chi xu ly 1 tai khoan voi 1 profile dang hoat dong.
- Khong chay nhieu tai khoan cung luc.

- Next:
  di tai khoan moi tu ds.txt

- Chay / Mo dong:
  mo lai tai khoan da co tren grid

- Xoa 1 dong:
  xoa du lieu quan ly cua tai khoan do
  dong thoi tra profile ve trang thai profile ranh


10. NHUNG PHAN CAN CODE TRUOC
-----------------------------

10.1. Phan ds.txt
- Neu chua co ds.txt thi tao moi.
- Doc file ds.txt.
- Lay dong dau tien.
- Tach uid va password.
- Neu file rong thi thong bao va mo file de nguoi dung nhap.
- Tim va xoa dung dong can xoa.

10.2. Phan profile
- Xac dinh thu muc `profile_mau`.
- Xac dinh thu muc `profile_ranh`.
- Neu co `profile_ranh` thi dung lai.
- Neu khong co thi clone moi tu `profile_mau`.
- Doi ten profile thanh UID.
- Khi xoa 1 dong thi reset profile va doi ve `profile_ranh`.

10.3. Phan grid
- Them dong moi khi bam Next.
- Tu dong chon dong moi vua tao.
- Luu thong tin toi thieu:
  UID
  Password
  ProfileName
  Ngay tao

10.4. Phan mo Chrome
- Mo dung user-data-dir cua profile.
- Mo dung giao dien Facebook da chon.
- Dien san uid va password 1 lan.

10.5. Phan khoa/mo nut
- Dieu khien Next, Chay, Xoa dong, menu theo so luong dong dang chon.

10.6. Phan xoa dong
- Xoa tren grid.
- Xoa trong ds.txt.
- Reset profile ve `profile_ranh`.


11. NHUNG PHAN TAM THOI CHUA CAN LAM
------------------------------------
- Tu dong kiem tra DOM de biet tai khoan doi mat khau.
- Tu dong xac nhan dang nhap thanh cong.
- Xu ly nhieu profile cung luc.
- Xoa nhieu dong kem logic xoa hang loat profile.
- Toi uu giao dien dep.
- Tich hop logic VPN phuc tap ngoai extension.


12. THU TU HOC VA CODE
----------------------
- Buoc 1:
  chot du lieu cot loi cua 1 dong tren grid.

- Buoc 2:
  lam phan ds.txt.

- Buoc 3:
  lam logic `profile_mau` va `profile_ranh`.

- Buoc 4:
  lam nut Next.

- Buoc 5:
  lam nut Chay / Mo dong.

- Buoc 6:
  lam khoa/mo nut theo so dong duoc chon.

- Buoc 7:
  lam xoa 1 dong + reset profile.

- Buoc 8:
  moi gan logic dien uid/password vao Facebook.


13. NGUYEN TAC PHAT TRIEN
------------------------
- Lam dung tung buoc nho.
- Moi buoc xong phai chay thu.
- Khong gop qua nhieu logic vao 1 lan.
- Uu tien dung nghiep vu truoc, dep sau.
- App nay la ban nang cap logic hon tao_profile, nhung van phai giu cach lam vua suc.
